## Abstraction of repo 

This is a learning system living under the @Learn folder. The problems to solve and corresponding goals are the following.
 1. Information explosion leading to overload, decision fatigue, and FOMO.
	- **Goal** : Automize process of source filtering by LLMs, return only worthy information.
2. Often lose track of knowledge and understanding in the past.
	- **Goal** : Keep track of notes I have made and reasoning process while learning.
3. Note-keeping itself is tedious and time-consuming yet significantly important.
	- **Goal** : Design easy to use and fast user interface for note taking. 

Given the ability LLM provides, text and language based systems and functions can be developed and automized easier, which led to the possibility of this project. 
Learning is the process of understanding, utilizing, and internalizing. However, a lot of friction is introduced during this process, taking up time and energy, for instance, collecting resource, and keeping track of notes. The ultimate goal of this system is to reduce as much friction as possible, and keep the system transparent for user's to understand and participate in each decision-making step.
