> **Claude:** You just described an **Inbox** — a place to throw things that need attention but don't belong anywhere yet. Simplest version: a single file like `Inbox.md` at the project root (or vault root), just a bullet list. When you have a random thought/problem: open Inbox, add a line, move on. During your next planning/organizing session, you process the inbox — move items to the right task page, scratchpad, or delete them. This is basically the GTD "capture → process" loop. The inbox IS your reminder — you just need the habit of checking it when you start a session.
